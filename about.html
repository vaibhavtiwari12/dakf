About Us

DAKSFIRST

is a mid-market focussed private investment firm working on identifying niche areas of credit mispricing driven by operational issues, economies of scale, legal/structure framework or lack of relevant data. We aim to address these risks using innovative ways by combining available data with heuristic frameworks to create value for our investors.

Daksfirst Limited is an appointed representative (registration number: 937220) of Maystone Capital Limited which is authorized and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FRN 758412). Daksfirst Limited is registered in England with a Company no. 11626401, VAT No. 373672277. Registered Office: 8, Hill Street London, England, W1J5NG.

Daksfirst has launched three funds with a specific mandate on geographies:

Daksfirst - CaymanFund set up in Cayman Island to focus on Emerging and Frontier Markets managed under the aegis of Daksfirst Limited with specific mandate to manage Emerging and Frontier Markets funds. Cayman fund has a target fund size of $ 100mn.

Daksfirst - LuxembourgLUXEMBOURG Fund set up in Luxembourg which act as a feeder vehicle to the Indian AIF and other European Investments with segregated compartments for different investors.

Daksfirst - IndiaCategory II Alternate Investment Fund registered with SEBI. Daksfirst India Special Situations Fund I has a fund size of ~ $200mn.
